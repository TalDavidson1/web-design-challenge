Latitude vs. Windspeed

Much like cloudiness vs latitude, windspeed affects most ranges of latitude. The areas with the greatest levels of variance seem to be from around 50 to 70, while overall windspeed is pretty univerasl for all other latitudes. That said, below around -50 there are very few measurements for windspeed.
