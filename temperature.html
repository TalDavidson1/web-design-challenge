Latitude vs. Temperature

While many of the visuals obtained during this project have faint patterns, temperature is the only one with a very distinct pattern. The closer to the equator you are, the higher the temperature (as expected). Closer to the poles, the cooler the temperatures. At the time of this project, the southern hemisphere (-60 to 0) shows higher temperatures due to the tilt of earth's axis. It would be interesting to do the project again during a different time of year, just to see the changes in temperature related to that tilt.
